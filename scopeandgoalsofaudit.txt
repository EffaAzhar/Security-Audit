#Risk Assessment
Risk assessment in auditing is a crucial process where auditors identify and evaluate potential risks that could affect the accuracy and reliability of an organization's financial statements or the effectiveness of its internal controls. Risk assessment is crucial for effectiveness,efficiency,and better audit planning. Auditors first try to identify potential areas where things might be wrong. This helps to focus on efforts and resources where they're most needed.
